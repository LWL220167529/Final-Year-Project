3 days trip in Tokyo

  01-02-2024 - 03-02-2024

Tradition collides with pop culture in Tokyo, where you can reverently wander ancient temples before rocking out at a karaoke bar. Wake up before the sun to catch the lively fish auction at the Toyosu Market, then refresh with a walk beneath the cherry blossom trees that line the Sumida River. Don’t forget to eat as much sushi, udon noodles, and wagashi (Japanese sweets) as your belly can handle.

Day 1

Morning

  Begin your day at Sensō-ji, the oldest temple in Tokyo. Enter through the Kaminarimon (Thunder Gate), notable for its giant red lantern.

  Take the Tokyo Metro Ginza Line to Asakusa Station.

Afternoon

  After a 30-minute walk or a short ride on the Tokyo Metro, reach Ueno to explore the Tokyo National Museum. Delve into Japan's history with exhibits like samurai armor and antique pottery.

  Walk or take the Tokyo Metro Ginza Line to Ueno Station.

Evening

  Spend the rest of the day in Ueno Park. Visit Shinobazu Pond, especially during the lotus blooming season.

  Everything is within walking distance in Ueno Park.

Day 2

Morning

  Start at Tokyo DisneySea, a unique Disney park. Explore areas like Mediterranean Harbor and Mysterious Island.

  Take the JR Keiyo Line to Maihama Station and transfer to the Disney Resort Line.

Afternoon

  After DisneySea, visit Shinjuku Gyoen, one of Tokyo's largest gardens. It features Japanese, French, and English landscapes.

  Take the JR Yamanote Line to Shinjuku Station, followed by a short walk.

Evening

  Visit Meiji Shrine, dedicated to Emperor Meiji and Empress Shoken. Participate in purification rituals at the temizuya and write wishes on an ema.

  A short walk from Shinjuku Gyoen or take the JR Yamanote Line to Harajuku Station.

Day 3

Morning

  Begin your day at TeamLab Borderless, a unique digital art museum that offers an immersive, futuristic experience.

  Take the Yurikamome Line from Shimbashi Station to Odaiba Station.

Afternoon

  Visit Hamarikyu Gardens, a beautiful Edo-period garden surrounded by skyscrapers, featuring seawater ponds and a traditional tea house.

  Take the Yurikamome Line back towards Shimbashi and get off at Shiodome Station, then walk to the gardens.

Evening

  Conclude your day in Ginza, Tokyo's premier shopping and entertainment district. Explore high-end shops, art galleries, and theaters.

  Walk from Hamarikyu Gardens to Ginza or take a short taxi ride.
